Fry Bread Recipe

Fry Bread From Scratch

This recipe will provide the batter needed to make delicious fry bread from scratch.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of flour
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1 tbsp of baking powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  1. Mix all ingredients at the same time and let rise for 1 hour.
  2. Heat canola oil on medium-high heat in cast iron skillet or deep pan.
  3. Break off pieces of dough and roll on a floured surface to desired size.
  4. Place in hot oil until golden brown flipping half way through.
  5. Will make approximately 10 medium size pieces.
  6. Add toppings and enjoy.

Pancake Recipe

Pancake Batter From Scratch

This recipe will provide the batter needed to make delicious pancakes from scratch.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/4 cup of flour
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 1/8 cup of buttermilk
    • (or mix 1 tbsp of vinegar or lemon juice and 1 cup of milk to make your own buttermilk)
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1 egg
  1. If making your own buttermilk mix the vinegar or lemon juice and milk and set aside.
  2. Combine all dry ingredients.
  3. Combine all wet ingredients including buttermilk mixture.
  4. Slowly add wet ingredients to dry.
  5. Mix until just combined, do not overmix, the butter will be slightly lumpy.
  6. Grease pan or griddle.
  7. Heat to medium-high heat.
  8. Add batter in batches according to desired size
  9. Will make approximately 6 large or 24 silver dollar size pancakes.
  10. Add toppings and enjoy.
